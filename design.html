Common Questions

Things people ask before reaching out.

Do I need to have everything ready before we start?

Not at all. I've started projects with nothing more than a business name and a general idea. If you're missing content, a logo, or a clear direction — I'll help you figure it out. That's part of what you're paying for.

Will I be able to update the site myself?

Yes. I build sites on platforms that are easy to manage — and I walk you through everything before handing it over. If something ever comes up, I'm an email away.

How long does a typical project take?

Most small-business sites take 2–4 weeks from kickoff to launch. Timelines depend on scope and how quickly we get feedback from you — but I'll always give you a clear estimate upfront.

What if I already have a site and just want it improved?

Redesigns and modernizations are a big part of what I do. I'll audit your current site, identify what's holding you back, and give you a clear plan before we touch anything.

Do you handle hosting and domains?

I can help you set everything up or work with what you already have. Either way, I take care of the technical side so you don't have to worry about it.

What's it actually like to work with you?

Direct and communicative. You'll know where things stand, what's coming next, and how to reach me. No waiting a week for a response. No getting handed off to a stranger.
